Use a color scheme to avoid clashing colors and hues. Combine dark and light colors for background and text for readibility.

Whenever possible, use an image to replace words. It conveys the message quickly, beautifully and universally.

San serif fonts are more modern and increase legibility and accessibility. Use as few fonts as possible on a page, never exceeding 3.

Use whitespace to signal importance of items on the page and create a logical flow on your webpage. Add 25% more white space than you think you need.
